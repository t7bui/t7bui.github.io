DSGN100 (UCSD - WI23) - Assignment Description

Students will create a poster for lifestyle festival.
You will be drawing inspiration from the Wonderfruit 2022, wonderfruit.co.

Learning about Wonderfruit:

Wonderfruit is a lifestyle festival that happens at Pattaya, Thailand on December 15th-18th.
People around the world who have interest or passion in music, art, culture, and nature come together to this event to have fun
and give back to nature.
The event encourange Carbon restoration and avoid single-use items. "The millions of kilometers it takes to move equipment,
resources, staff and musicians to Wonderfruit from around the world contribute more than 1,300 tons of CO2 to the atmosphere."
