DSGN100 (UCSD - WI23) - Assignment Description

Students will create a poster for lifestyle festival.
You will be drawing inspiration from the Wonderfruit 2022, wonderfruit.co.

Learning about Wonderfruit:

Wonderfruit is a lifestyle festival that happens at Pattaya, Thailand on December 15th-18th.
People around the world who have interest or passion in music, art, culture, and nature come together to this event to have fun and give back to nature.
The event encourange Carbon restoration and avoid single-use items. "The millions of kilometers it takes to move equipment, resources, staff and musicians to Wonderfruit from around the world contribute more than 1,300 tons of CO2 to the atmosphere."

My goal for this poster:

Since this is a festival where people around the world come together to share their love and passion in music, art, culture, and nature, it would be interesting to make the poster look fun from afar and somehow incorporate a heart using Text type as Image learned from class reading.
Another thing that is I want to emphasize on the poster is the important of single-use items and Carbon restoration.
