Why do I Enjoy Designing

Designing, to me, is not only a means of communication but also a way to connect with individuals who possess diverse backgrounds and perspectives.

This belief was reinforced during my transition to the U.S. in 6th grade when I faced language and culture barriers. With limited English skills, I struggled to communicate with my classmates. Fortunately, I met a mutual friend who had recently transferred from a different elementary school. Despite the communication challenges, we were able to break that barrier through art. We expressed our emotions and thoughts through our drawings and laughed at those silly artwork 😅 She patiently taught the English translations for the things we drew, and thanks to her, my English skills improved tremendously. This experience highlighted the power of design as a universal language, allowing for meaningful connections to be formed.
